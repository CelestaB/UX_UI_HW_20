About Me

I’m Celesta

UX/UI designer. Artist. Art Director. Dancer

I come from a very arts heavy background. I’m also very keen on technology, organization, and design. I have found that UX design allows me to utilize my previous training and visual skills.

Due to my arts background and playful imagination, my work is slightly unorthodox which leads to an innovative and distinctive design.

UX Design

Leading with my user-centered mindset, paired with my ability to problem solve and open mind when collaborating with clients, I have found that I excel in user research.

UI and Development

A dedication to detail, persistence, and thoroughness has proven itself exceptionally useful in UI. I thrive on detail-oriented tasks such as redlining, prototyping, and developing.

Art Direction

In ways such as directing a theatrical production from beginning to end, I have found myself in leadership roles. A strength is being able to visualize and execute a project from an original concept to a final product.
